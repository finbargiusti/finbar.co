I've been working at Stripe for almost a year full-time. In that time I've
learned a lot about payments, startups, what works at scale and what doesn't.

I've worked only in the Authentication team. We deal with cardholder
authentication - simply put verifying that the cardholder at the time of
payment is who they _say_ they are.
